



Ahammed Fazle Rabbi (born 8 December 1980), known popularly as Afran Nisho,[1] is a Bangladeshi actor, story writer and playback singer. He won Meril Prothom Alo Awards for critics' choice Best TV Actor for his role in the TV drama Jog Biyog in (2016) and he wrote story and singing debut for director Kajal Arefin Ome's TV drama Muthophone (2019).[2][3] He also won Meril Prothom Alo Awards for Best TV Actor for his role in Punorjonmo (2021). He made his debut in film as a lead in Surongo (2023) directed by Raihan Rafi.[4]

Personal life
Nisho was born in Bhuapur, Tangail, Bangladesh on 8 December 1980.[5] He married Trisha in 2011 and they have one child.[6]

Career
Nisho began his career as a model in a television commercial in 2003.[7] Nisho has worked in countless TV dramas, telefilms and TV series and has done around 850+ TV works for countless TV channels. He has worked with celebrities such as Mehazabien Chowdhury, Tanjin Tisha, Aupee Karim, Ziaul Faruq Apurba, Tahsan Rahman Khan, Chanchal Chowdhury, Tasnia Farin, Nusrat Imrose Tisha and many more. He has worked in countless adverts before entering in as an actor. TVCs such as AKTEL goon goon and pre-paid, Gazi Tyres etc.

In 2023, Nisho made his debut in Bangladeshi film industry with Raihan Rafi's crime thriller Surongo,[8] which received generally positive reviews and became a major commercial success in Box office.[9] Following a brief hiatus, he reunited with Alpha-i, Chorki and SVF in Shihab Shaheen's Crime/Drama Daagi in 2025.[10]
