Trim() ----> Removes first and last white spaces from string like "  Mahfuz   " to "Mahfuz"
TrimStart() --> Removes first white spaces from string like "  Mahfuz" to "Mahfuz"
TrimEnd()---> Removes last white spaces from string like "Mahfuz   " to "Mahfuz"
ToLower() ---> Converts the strings to lowercase 
ToUpper() ----> Converts the strings to Uppercase
